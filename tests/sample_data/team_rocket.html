Team Rocket

Team Rocket (Japanese: ロケット団だん Rocket-dan, literally Rocket Gang) is a villainous team operating in the Pokémon world, most notably in the Kanto region, with a small outpost in the Sevii Islands.

The trio often attempts elaborate schemes to steal rare Pokémon, notably Pikachu, but their plans are frequently thwarted. Despite repeated failures, Team Rocket remains persistent.

Many encounters involve battles with trainers, encounters with legendary species, and comic relief moments.
