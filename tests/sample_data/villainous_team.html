Villainous team

A villainous team (Japanese: 悪の組織 evil organization) is a group of antagonists with a goal in opposition to the player and other friendly characters. Each generation of the core series games features a villainous team as a major plot element.

Notable teams include Team Rocket from Kanto, Team Aqua and Team Magma from Hoenn, and Team Galactic from Sinnoh.

These teams often attempt to capture legendary Pokémon to achieve their goals. The player character typically battles team members throughout their journey and ultimately confronts the team leader.

Despite their villainous nature, some teams have members who question their organization's methods. Many teams disband after their leader is defeated by the player.
